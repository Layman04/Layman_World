Who We are


Nexus Corporate Solution Pvt. Ltd. an organization incorporated in 2012, by two young entrepreneurs striving to provide customized HR solutions, IT Solutions & Healthcare practices to companies worldwide, with the flexibility to meet their distinct needs and have a mission to help fulfill the dreams of their clients.

Nexus Corporate Solution to Boost Your Creative Projects

Nexus Corporate Solution Pvt Ltd services kicked off with a small team of bright entrepreneurs striving to disrupt the prevalent paradigm of Human Resource Management And Digital Marketing practices worldwide. In the last Ten years, we have served a number of clients from a variety of industries catering to their unique personal requirements. At Nexus Corporate Solution Pvt Ltd, our mantra is to intricately analyze our clients and provide them a customized solution rather than following a one-size-fits-all approach. Despite being a young company, we have emerged as a preferred consulting partner for multinational firms and prominent business houses across the Indian subcontinent. Our roots in HR consulting have allowed us to tackle the challenges of business process management related to the front, back and middle office. Our spectrum of services include consulting, recruitment and software development services across various industry segments. We are in the business of creating a secure and rewarding future for our clients and their employees.
